## [1.0.0] - 2025-XX-XX

### Added
- **Stump Block** - Craftable from 3 logs, used for chopping wood
- **Firewood Item** - Obtained by chopping logs on stumps
- **Firewood Stack Block** - Placeable firewood that stacks up to 4 pieces
- **Unlit Campfire Block** - Created by adding coal to a full firewood stack
- Ability to light unlit campfires with Flint and Steel or Fire Charge
- Full integration with vanilla campfire mechanics

### Features
- Immersive multi-step campfire creation process
- Visual firewood stacking system
- Compatible with all vanilla log types
- Works alongside vanilla campfire crafting
